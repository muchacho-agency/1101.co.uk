Network Security

Most companies take nearly 6 months to detect a data breach, even major ones.

Why do you need network security?

Network security is a complex and ever-changing aspect of IT that is laced with threat from unsolicited cyber attacks. Holes in the network leave your entire computing system at risk of exploitation of its critical systems. So, are you confident your network is robust enough?.

If a network is architecturally and technically designed, this should provide adequate protection and integrity against internal and external threats. Firewalls, VPNs, antivirus and network access control are the first line of defence.

But experienced IT engineers, such as those at 1101, will always advise on additional barriers of protection, such as segregated networks, secure wireless access, administrative authorisation, and regular testing.

We can manage your network’s perimeter to monitor access points, protocols and applications, as well as filtering and inspecting any traffic to and from the network. By implementing simple processes and appropriate architecture, this can reduce the likelihood of attacks happening while also delivering technical responses if the worst happens.

Features

Advanced multi-layer protection

Cyber criminals are constantly finding new ways to infiltrate network. Stay one step ahead of them to protect your company’s digital assets and integrity at every layer of the network.

Stop attacks before they enter the network

Because we constantly monitor your network, we can detect and prevent any unauthorised access or modification before they can wreak havoc and cause costly downtime and disruption.

Secure data sharing between staff

Retain your privacy and compliance by ensuring vulnerable data is protected against attack when sent across the network to various endpoints.

Manage traffic more efficiently

Think of your network like a motorway: the more cars (endpoints), the slower you travel – and nobody likes being stuck in congestion. We can help make sure your expanding network runs smoothly and efficiently to improve user performance.

Disaster recovery options

Nobody or no system is infallible. Should a cyber attack happen despite continuous scanning and penetration testing, our managed network security process ensures any lost data is restored.

Centralised and simple process

Network security can be a complex process; we’ll manage every aspect to take away the stress from your team so you can focus on what you do best.
