Guiding Principles

1101 appreciates its responsibility to the individual, to others and to the environment. It endeavours to empower, enable, and facilitate and is committed to ensure:

  • Employees have the freedom and support to "have a go" to realise their ambition and achieve their true potential to deliver accurately, competently, honestly and with integrity.
  • Our Customers and Partners are treated respectfully and fairly at all times; delivering maximum benefit and value in all transactions.
  • We continue to grow and develop effectively; moving with the times and contributing to a sustainable environment; enjoying the journey and gaining respect and loyalty as we go.
