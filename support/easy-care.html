EasyCare

For the occasional “IT Fires” you need to put out

Why do you need easy-care?

If you’ve never enlisted the help of a managed service provider before, what’s behind your hesitation? If it’s the thought of being tied into a contract that you may not fully utilise, then have you considered a pay-as-you-go service?

As we all know from the films, disasters happen when you least expect them to, or worse still when you’re least prepared. Having a PAYG IT service plan in place is like having emergency breakdown cover for if the worst should happen. We’re the recovery guys you call out to rescue you if disaster strikes and you’re up the creek without a paddle.

Many new clients who aren’t 100 percent sure they need on-going IT support choose to test the water by pre-purchasing a set amount of support hours that they can use as and when necessary.

This could be used for things like desktop support for virus or malware removal, one-off projects like cloud migration, or unexpected, critical issues like server failures or data breaches.

PAYG clients still get the same level of support from our expert team of engineers, but with costs that reflect their specific and personalised needs.
